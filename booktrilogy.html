book trilogy

For this project I was assigned the task of designing three book covers inspired by the narratives of the “This is Criminal” podcast, hosted by Phoebe Judge, with the intention of creating a trilogy. I chose to listen to “How to Sell A Haunted House,” “Ian Manuel,” and “Sanctuary” as the episodes to base my designs on.

software

Illustrator InDesign Photoshop

year

2021

I began by listening to each podcast, taking notes, brainstorming ideas, and sketching out my designs. In this stage I didn't worry about how I'll be tying each book together. I wanted to ensure that the designs helped convey what each story may be about.

After narrowing down my sketches to one per book I began experimenting with different mediums for one design to find what would work best.
