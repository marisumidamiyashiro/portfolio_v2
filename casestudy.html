book trilogy

For this project I was assigned the task of designing three book covers inspired by the narratives of the “This is Criminal” podcast, hosted by Phoebe Judge, with the intention of creating a trilogy. I chose to listen to “How to Sell A Haunted House,” “Ian Manuel,” and “Sanctuary” as the episodes to base my designs on.

software

Illustrator InDesign Photoshop

year

2021

I began this project by listening to each podcast, taking notes, and brainstorming ideas. I then began sketching out my ideas based on the information I had gathered. In this stage I didn't worry about how I would be tying each book cover together. Instead, I wanted to ensure that each design conveyed what the story might be about.

After narrowing down my sketches to one design per book, I chose to focus on one story and experiment with different media. I made sure to keep my options open and play around before finalizing the medium I'd work in for the last two books.

I chose to create each book cover using clay, and proceeded to craft the final two objects I'd need. I then went into Photoshop to touch up the images, and used Illustrator and InDesign to finalize the overall layout.

A unified book trilogy had been achieved using sculpted clay figures, color, type, and the overall layout. “How To Sell A Haunted House” served as the foundation, upon which I crafted the covers for “Ian Manuel” and “Sanctuary,” utilizing a consistent design concept.
